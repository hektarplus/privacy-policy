Privacy Policy for Hectar GPS Field Area Measure
Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Hectar GPS Field Area Measure (developed by TOPGNSS) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use
Location Data (Prominent Disclosure):
Our App requires access to your location data to provide core features such as field area measurement, perimeter calculation, and track recording.

Background Location: The App collects location data even when the app is closed or not in use (in the background) if the "Track Recording" or "Field Measurement" mode is active. This is necessary to ensure an accurate and continuous measurement of your movement across the field while your screen is off or you are using other apps.

We only access location data after obtaining your explicit consent within the App.

Personal Data:

Google Account Information: If you choose to sign in via Google, we may receive your email address, name, and profile picture. This data is used solely for user authentication and to enable cloud synchronization for Premium features via Google Firebase.

2. Data Storage and Third-Party Services
Local Storage: By default, all measurement data is stored locally on your device.

Cloud Storage: For Premium users, data is synced to Google Firebase to allow access across multiple devices.

Payment Processing: All purchases are handled securely through Google Play Billing. We do not store or process your credit card information.

Maps & Ads: We use Google Maps for mapping and Google AdMob for displaying advertisements in the free version.

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data or location history with third parties for marketing or any other commercial purposes. Data is only shared with the Google services mentioned above to ensure the App's functionality.

4. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion
You can delete your saved field data within the App at any time. If you wish to delete your account or synced cloud data, you can do so through the App settings or by contacting us.
